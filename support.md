# Steady Blooming — Support

Welcome. A few common questions, then how to get in touch.

## How it works

**Habits become campfires.** Each habit you add starts a fire in
your camp. Tend it daily (tap the campsite → "Yes, did it ✓") and
the flame deepens. Skip a few days and it dims — but the campfire
stays. Pick it back up and the flame brightens again.

**Goals become plants.** Each goal you add plants a seed. Break the
goal into small steps; each step you complete nudges the tree
taller. When the goal is finished, the tree stays in your camp permanently.

**Just show up.** No streaks to break, no graphs to maintain, no
push to optimize anything.

## Common questions

**Q: How do I add a habit or goal?**
Tap the **+** button in the top-right corner of the camp. Pick
"Light a campfire" (habit) or "Plant a seed" (goal).

**Q: I missed a day. Is my progress reset?**
No. Your campfires and plants don't get destroyed by missed days
— they just dim slightly. Tend them again and they brighten right
back up.

**Q: How do I set a reminder?**
When adding a habit or goal, tap the bell icon to set a daily
reminder time. To change or remove one later, tap an existing
campsite or plant → adjust the reminder in the dialog.

**Q: Where is my data stored? Is it backed up?**
All your data lives on your device only — there's no cloud sync
and no server copy. To preserve your camp across devices or after
reinstalling, make sure your iPhone is backed up to iCloud (Settings
→ [your name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup).

**Q: Why aren't my notifications appearing?**
Check **Settings → Notifications → Steady Blooming** and make sure
notifications are allowed.

**Q: I want to delete a habit, goal, or completed tree.**
Tap any campsite, plant, or tree to bring up the dialog, then pick
the "Extinguish / Uproot / Cut down" option.
