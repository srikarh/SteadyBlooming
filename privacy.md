# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 26, 2026

Steady Blooming is built with privacy as the default, not a feature.
Here's exactly what happens to your data.

## What we collect

**Nothing leaves your device.** Steady Blooming has no accounts, no
sign-in, no cloud sync, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no
third-party services of any kind.

Specifically:
- Your habits, goals, completion history, and reminder times are
  stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- AI-generated text in the app (camp descriptions, notification
  bodies) is generated entirely on-device by Apple's Foundation
  Models — your data is never sent to a server.
- Notifications are scheduled locally by iOS and never transmitted
  anywhere.

## What we don't do

- We do not collect personal information.
- We do not track you across apps or websites.
- We do not sell, rent, share, or transmit your data.
- We do not use cookies, fingerprinting, or any tracking technology.
- We do not have access to anything you enter in the app.

## Permissions

Steady Blooming may ask for:

- **Notifications** — so it can remind you of habits and goals you've
  set reminders for. You can revoke this at any time in iOS
  Settings → Notifications → Steady Blooming.

That is the only permission the app requests.

## Data retention and deletion

All your data lives on your device. Deleting the app from your iPhone
deletes all your data permanently. There is no server copy, no backup
in our possession, nothing to "request deletion of" because we don't
have it.

## Children

Steady Blooming is appropriate for users of all ages. We do not
knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because we
do not collect data from anyone.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will appear at this
